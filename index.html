DUCK SLAYER

GLOBAL GAME JAM 2025 (Bremen) 1st place. Read more

TRY IT OUT

Surf your way to cleanliness in Duck Slayer! Control a bubbly figure gliding on a soap bar through a chaotic bathtub. Jump and dash your way to clean up the mess, defeating waves of pesky rubber ducks to refill your soap supply and clear the grime. Use sponges, toothbrushes, ramps, and more to string together stylish combos for explosive cleaning power. The bigger your combo, the more sparkling clean the bathtub becomes. Rack up points by cleaning as much as you can before the soap runs out!

This game was created during the global game jam 2025. All Assets including Music & Sound were either created from scratch or modified from free to use sources. We used Godot for the implementation and leveldesign, Blender for 3D-assets and Soundtrap + Audacity for music & sfx.

WOODWORK WITH COBOTS

My Bachelor Thesis "Woodwork with cobots".
This was made as part of the research project "KIWI" from the BIME (University of Bremen), ITB and apps-lab. Read more

Abstract

This thesis addresses the integration of collaborative robots (cobots) into the craft industry, specifically within the woodworking contexts. The primary objective is to develop and evaluate a conceptual framework for a virtual reality (VR) application designed to simulate interactions with cobots during woodworking tasks. The application allows users to engage with cobots in cushioning a chair. This interaction involves following instructional prompts, engaging with the cobot sequentially, and integrating its assistance to complete the task. Evaluation of the proposed concept focuses on technical functionality and user experience, utilizing the System Usability Scale (SUS) to assess the application's usability. Results show that the VR application is technically working and has good usability according to the SUS. However, further evaluation is needed to assess the practical use of the proposed concept, as insights from industry professionals are limited. Thus, additional research and engagement with stakeholders are needed to determine the ultimate potential of integrating cobots into woodworking practices.

For this Project I have used several software and hardware including: Unreal Engine 5, Blender, Photoshop, Audacity, Megascans & Oculus Meta Quest 2

META VIEW

The project created as part of the METAVERSED! - IMMERSIVE HACKATHON 2022 is an immersive type of presentation that allows us to show an audience worlds as they have never experienced them before.

As a “tour guide”, one person takes control in VR and has the opportunity to “take the audience by the hand”, showing them what's inside a world while never losing the context of the environment.

While one person guides the journey, the audience sees the worlds in a fulldome projection and can point out things worth seeing and give directions to the tour guide.

FORGOTTEN DARKNESS

Forgotten Darkness is a third-person horror game in which the player takes on the role of a man man who, damaged by amnesia, tries to find out where he is. On his way to bring light into the darkness, he learns what kind of terrible house he finds himself in...

The music and the sound is adapted to the protagonist, who is a piano teacher and therefore has a strong influence on the music. Furthermore, Forgotten Darkness lives from its atmosphere and gloomy appearance, as well as the antagonist.

This was my first "big" project with the Unreal Engine 4. Besides the UE we used alot of different tools and tried out many new things like Metahumans.
My focus was on creating a dark scary atmosphere by leveldesign, using vfx, sfx and post processing.

SPACE ADVENTURE

The space explorer glides through space, swimming through time and space, always on the lookout for new friends and exciting adventures.

On the way to a planet she has found, mysterious mysterious things fly by, will she manage to capture them?

PIECES OF THE SOUNDTRACK:

Our outer space has many fascinating places that are now closer than ever to reach. Whether planets like Mars, stars or other mysterious objects. Space offers a multitude of topics relevant to research. In addition to the positive, fascinating things that exist in space, arise problems that we humans have brought into space. One of these ever-growing problems is the space debris created by our spaceships, satellites and other products. products. Space Adventure addresses this problem and tries to solve it in an in an interesting and perhaps amusing story.
